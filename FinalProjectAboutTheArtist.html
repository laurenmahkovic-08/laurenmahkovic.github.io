About the Artist


Hi! My name is Lauren Mahkovic, I am a 20 year old artist based out of Greensburg, Pennsylvania. I am currenly a college student studying geology, biology, and museum studies, all of which heavily influence my artistic work. I primarily do digital work but also commonly use ink, watercolor, and lino-cut block prints. I have competed in several art competitions in my home town and have recived a few awards for my work. Additionally I have also had my work featured in a gallery for the Southern Allegheny Museum of Art in Ligonere, PA. My artwork is heavily infulenced by my passion for science and nature, I have done several paleoartistic illustraions for personal and academic use. A website that I commonly use for refrences in my scientific illustraion is the Univerity of California Berkeley invertebrate zoology collection. An additonal infulence for my work is the Czech arist Alphonz Mucha, a pioneer of the art nouvue movement from the 1920's, examples of his work can be here. Block printing is an artisitc tradtion found across the world for thousands of years, but isn't as popular in modern works as compared to other mediums. It quickly became one of my favorite ways to create reproducable works of art, a great resource to learn more about block printing can be found at the Metropolitan Museum of Art's website.
