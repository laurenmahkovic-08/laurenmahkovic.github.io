Tarot Card Back

Digital 2025

This is the design for the back of the tarot deck I have been designing as a longterm project. Eventually I am hoping to have a full illustrated deck in my own style to have printed with this as the back. It features a representation of each suit of the minor arcana and the eye in center represnts the act of divination itself which many people use tarot cards for.


The Fool

Digital 2025

The Fool is the first card in the major arcana. Traditionally the fool is a jovial court jester about to walk off a cliff and into the crashing waves of the ocean, I depicted her here as more of a sly playful character.


The Magician

Digital 2024

The Magician is the second card in the major arcana, and the first one I drew, and usually depicts a man with a wand and the infinity symbol above his head to represnt power. I incorperated elements of this design into my card. In my work the Magician is also depicted with the infinity, and she is shown using magic.
